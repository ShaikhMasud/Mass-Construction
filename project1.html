Project Details

Location: Jawahar Deep 4,BPT, Near Bahucha Dhaka, Mumbai

Painting on the port side of a bridge refers to applying protective coatings or paint to the left side (when facing forward) of the bridge structure. This maintenance activity prevents corrosion, enhances durability, and improves the aesthetic appearance of the bridge. It typically involves surface preparation, priming, and applying paint in layers.
