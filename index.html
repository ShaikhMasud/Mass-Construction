Samad Shaikh

Civil Contractor

Experience

With over 24 years of experience in the construction industry, I bring a wealth of knowledge and hands-on expertise to every project I undertake. My journey began in 2000 when I started working with Saporgi Paloungi Company and later expanded my skills with renowned organizations like Mahindra Company and Haware as a civil contractor. In 2002, I enhanced my professional portfolio by collaborating with Simplex Company and Reliance Company, contributing to a variety of ambitious projects. Over the years, I have built a strong reputation for delivering excellence and currently work with leading companies such as Ratnashree Company, Royal Works Company, and Hindustan Company. My areas of expertise include: Painting and Plaster Work, Waterproofing Solutions, Reinforced Cement Concrete (RCC) Work, Brickwork and Masonry, Tile Installation and Finishing, With a steadfast commitment to quality and a proven track record, I strive to provide exceptional results, ensuring every project meets and exceeds client expectations.
